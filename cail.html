About Us


The research group, Cognitive Agents and Interaction Lab (CAIL), has been initiated to conduct research in various domains of broader Artificial Intelligence. We are specifically interested in developing distributed decision-making algorithms to deal with constraints in cooperative or competitive multi-agent systems that involve either or both human and artificial agents. We also study the practicality of these algorithms while deploying on large real-world applications such as disaster response, citizen science, sensor networks, target tracking, etc.
